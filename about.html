About Cool2Fun

Free HTML5 games, playable anywhere.

Cool2Fun is a free online games portal. Every game runs directly in your web browser using modern HTML5 technology, so there is nothing to download and nothing to install. Open a game and start playing in seconds — on your desktop, laptop, tablet or phone.

What we offer

  • A hand-picked collection of arcade, puzzle, racing, casual and sports games.
  • Instant play with no sign-up required.
  • A clean, fast, mobile-friendly experience.

Our mission

We believe games should be easy to reach and fun to play. Our goal is to bring together quality browser games in one simple, distraction-light place that loads quickly and works on any device.
